# TOGS & DOGS: SERVICE & SUPPORT SUMMARY

Hi Ryan,

This document summarizes the ongoing support and infrastructure model for your pet sitting application. Our goal is to keep your platform secure, stable, and cost-effective as you grow your business.

---

## **1. Monthly Billing Model**
Your monthly invoice will consist of two primary parts:

### **A. AWS Infrastructure (Pass-Through)**
We host your application on Amazon Web Services (AWS). To ensure you only pay for what you use, we pass these costs through to you at our **actual cost** with **no markup**.
- **Typical Range**: Expect $10 – $30 per month depending on your customer volume.
- **Monitoring**: We have an automated budget alert set for $20.00 to ensure there are no surprises.

### **B. Maintenance & Support Retainer**
**Fee**: $75.00 / month
This fee covers the proactive work required to keep the site running smoothly:
- **Hosting Management**: We handle all the cloud infrastructure "under the hood."
- **Security Updates**: Ensuring your user data and admin login remain secure.
- **Core Stability**: Standard bug fixes and performance monitoring.

---

## **2. Additional Services**
For work specifically requested beyond standard maintenance, the following rates apply:

### **Standard Change Requests**
- **Rate**: $95.00 / hour
- **Usage**: Small updates, text changes, or logic refinements.
- **Approval**: We will always give you an estimate and get your approval before starting any work that will take more than 2 hours.

### **Emergency / After-Hours Support**
- **Rate**: $150.00 / hour
- **Usage**: Critical issues requested outside of standard business hours (Mon-Fri, 9 AM - 5 PM EST).

### **Feature Enhancements**
- For significant new features (e.g., new reporting modules or complex integrations), we will provide a separate fixed-price scope for your approval.
