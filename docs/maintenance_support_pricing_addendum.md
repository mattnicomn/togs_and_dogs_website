## **1. Purpose**
This addendum defines the recurring maintenance, support, and specialized service fees for the Togs & Dogs operational platform. It ensures the application remains secure, updated, and highly available for day-to-day pet sitting operations.

---

## **2. Standard Monthly Support Suite**
**Monthly Fee**: $75.00
This retainer covers the following proactive services:
- **Hosting Management**: Oversight of AWS S3, CloudFront, and Lambda infrastructure.
- **Security Guardrails**: Periodic patch review and monitoring for authentication integrity.
- **Bug Fixes**: Resolution of identified defects in the original system's core functionality.
- **Performance Spot-Checks**: Ensuring the Master Scheduler and Care Card load within baseline parameters.

---

## **3. Cloud Infrastructure Pass-Through**
**Fee**: Actual usage cost + 0% markup.
AWS infrastructure costs are passed directly to Ryan with no surcharge.
- **Typical Range**: $10.00 – $30.00 per month (depending on intake volume).
- **Transparency**: US Mission Hero monitors these via AWS Budgets and will notify Ryan if costs exceed the $20.00 baseline project budget.

---

## **4. Out-of-Scope & Professional Services**
**Hourly Rate**: $95.00/hr
Work that involves structural changes or new feature engineering is billed hourly.
- **New Feature Requests**: Adding new pages, custom intake logic, or reporting integrations.
- **Third-Party Migrations**: Moving data to different platforms or payment gateways.
- **Emergency After-Hours Support**: Assistance requested outside of 9 AM – 5 PM EST on weekdays may be subject to a premium rate ($150.00/hr).

---

## **5. Approval & Payment**
- **Routine Work**: Maintenance is performed automatically under the monthly retainer.
- **Non-Routine Work**: Any work estimated to exceed 2 hours ($150.00) requires written approval (Email/Text) before work begins.
- **Invoicing**: Sent on the [1st] of each month, due within [15] days.

---

## **6. Assumptions & Exclusions**
- **External API Fees**: Costs for third-party services (e.g., Google Maps, SMS providers) are outside this scope and billed to the client separately.
- **Data Entry**: Managing pet records and intake data is the responsibility of Togs & Dogs.
