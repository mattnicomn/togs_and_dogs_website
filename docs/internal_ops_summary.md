## **2. Monthly Checklist (1st–5th of Mo)**
1. **Cost Audit**:
   - Check the **Cost Explorer** saved view. 
   - Ensure you are grouping by **Service** to verify usage spikes.
   - Confirm total project cost is within the $20 baseline budget.
2. **System Health**:
   - Perform a quick login test to the admin dashboard.
   - Verify that the Master Scheduler renders current data.
3. **Invoicing**:
   - Fill the `client_invoice_fillable.md`.
   - transfer AWS pass-through costs (at actual rate).
   - Add the $75 standard retainer.
   - List any approved change requests from the previous month.
